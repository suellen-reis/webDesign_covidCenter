Self-Assessment

Take this self-assessment if you have any symptoms of illness or tested positive for COVID-19.

You will get a recommendation on what to do next.

The self-assessment is only meant as an aid and it is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Individuals who are unwell or experiencing any symptoms of illness, even if they are not listed in this self-assessment tool, are advised to remain at home and consult with a healthcare provider if necessary.

Consult a health care provider if you have medical questions.
