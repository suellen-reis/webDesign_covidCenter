COVID-19 Self-Assessment

Do you currently have any of the following symptoms?

  • Severe difficulty breathing (e.g., struggling for each breath, can only speak in single words)
  • Severe chest pain (e.g.,constant tightness or crushing sensation)
  • feeling confused or disoriented
  • Extreme fatigue (having a hard time waking up or staying awake)
  • Losing consciousness
  • Chronic health conditions that you are having difficulty managing because of your current respiratory illness
